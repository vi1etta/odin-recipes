The Perfect Peanut Butter Milkshake

Learn how to make a peanut butter milkshake with just three ingredients you are almost sure to have on hand for a delicious treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • Â¼ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter

How to cook:

  1. Prepear all ingredients
  2. Combine ice cream, milk, and peanut butter in a blender; blend until smooth, about 45 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!
